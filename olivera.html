Olivera

"I don't know where my songs come from. I just listen to the endless radio show inside my head and record the best parts of it. " Originally from Espoo, Finland, singer and songwriter Olivera is an interesting mixture of a modern indie pop artist and a goofy little garden gnome. On her productions she works with the Finnish producer Lenno. "In life things turn to shit pretty often. That’s why I named my first EP” Life Is a Blooper Reel”. But isn’t the blooper reel always the funniest part?” Olivera has also worked as a songwriter and a featuring artist for names like Tiësto, Zaxx, 3LAU, Sonny Alven, Lucian and John Dahlbäck. So far the songs she’s written have been streamed over 35 million times and covered by outlets such as Billboard, Forbes and Scandipop.
