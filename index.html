This is Ice flow

The Ice Flow is a project aimed at introducing the singers and songwriters of the Nordic Region to the rest of the world. We hope to bring you names you never heard of and to gain an insight into the talent that resides in the Nordic region

About

Ice Flow OUR MISSION IS SIMPLE This is a project designed to introduce some of the strongest music talent in the Nordic Region to the rest of the world. The site in particular is focused on the abilities of the female singer/songwriters, of which there are many amazing artists.
