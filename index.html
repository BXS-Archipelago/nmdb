This is Iceflow

The Ice Flow project aimed at introducing the singers and songwriters of the Nordic Region to the rest of the world. We hope to bring you names you never heard of and to gain an insight into the talent that resides in the Nordic region


You will find a comprehensive list of artists in our gallery. However, if you know of other artists we should feature, please contact us.

About

Ice Flow OUR MISSION IS SIMPLE This is a project designed to introduce some of the strongest music talent in the Nordic Region to the rest of the world. The site in particular is focused on the abilities of the female singer/songwriters, of which there are many amazing artists.
